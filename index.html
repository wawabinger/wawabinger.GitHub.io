About me
I am currently a Fourth-year Undergraduate with the School of Computer Science, Nanjing university of Posts and telecommunications (NJUPT). I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Guozi Sun. My research interests include privacy, blockchain, and federated learning. My current research project aims at decentralizing and securing federated learning with the help of blockchain.
